Intro

I am an aspiring materials scientist with a demonstrated history of working in experimental research. I have an ardent desire to develop novel nanomaterials and nanotechnology, so I am working to hone my skills in materials synthesis and processing, materials characterization, energy materials, and spintronics through my master’s research at Missouri State University. My bachelor’s degree in Materials and Metallurgical Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), the premier engineering school in Bangladesh, enabled me to grasp the core concepts of materials science and later inspired me to pursue a master’s degree in Materials Science.

In my free time, I love to try new baking dishes, go to gym, watch documentaries.
